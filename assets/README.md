## Design Guidelines

### App Icon
- Should represent "Friend Auction" concept
- Consider using: hearts, people icons, auction elements
- Use your app's pink color scheme (#ec4899)
- Keep it simple and recognizable at small sizes

### Splash Screen
- Should match your app's branding
- Use the pink gradient background
- Consider adding your app name/logo
- Keep loading time in mind
